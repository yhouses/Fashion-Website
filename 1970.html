1970's

Below is a picture of actress Margot Robbie on the set of 'Once Upon a Time in Hollywood'. Even though the picture was taken just three years ago in 2019, it could've easily been mistaken for someone in the 70's. Margot is wearing go-go boots, a white miniskirt, and a turtleneck: three key staples in the 70's.

Shown in the picture below is Cher. Also known as the 'Goddess of Pop', she was a big style icon in the 1970's. Her outfit consisting of bell-bottom jeans and an earth toned blouse gives off the bohemian-folk energy that was prevalent in the 70's.

A lot of 70's fashion originated from the 60's. Polka-dots and other retro patterns were popular, especially early in the decade.
