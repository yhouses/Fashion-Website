2000

What was fashion like in the early 2000's? 2000's fashion took much inspiration from 1960's bohemian looks, which consisted of yoga pants, low-rise jeans, baggy pants, cowl-neck shirt, flared pants, cropped jackets, crop tops, baby tees, and mini skirts. Along with the accessorization, of jeweled baker boy caps, belts, baguette bags, and some shiny jewelry, 2000's fashion was glamorous. It is often seen as an era of a global mash up, in which trends experienced the fusion of previous vintage styles, as well as the collaboration of global and ethnic clothing. Here in this picture, we can see one of 2000's fashion icons, Paris Hilton. She usually set the standard for fashion trends. Here we can see her wearing one of her iconic graphic tees with a mini skirt, and bright pink baguette bag.
