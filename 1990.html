1990

When thinking about 90's fashion icons, she is almost always the first person to come to mind. Diana, Princess of Wales was known to be a charitable and kindhearted person who had a remarkable sense of fashion. Princess Diana made chunky sneakers, biker shorts, and crewnecks look fashionable and put together; so we can thank Princess Diana for making this kind of comfortable athleisure trendy again.

Drew Barrymore was one of the most well known actresses in the 90's. In the picture below, she is wearing a mom jeans and sneakers that contrast nicely with her dainty polkadot blouse. This outfit has a perfevt mix of feminine and boyish notes.

Pictured below is actress, Zoe Kravits, wearing low rise baggy jeans and a cropped, black racerback; two very big trends in the 90s.
